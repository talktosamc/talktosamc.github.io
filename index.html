FAQs

How will we evaluate results?

We agree upfront on a clear, measurable target, and review progress regularly during a 60-day engagement. Iteration is expected, especially early on as things ramp up.

How are you compensated?

Compensation is typically split between a modest monthly retainer and a performance-based component tied to results.

Will I be locked into a contract?

No. Engagements typically run in 60-day intervals and are renewable, with an early-exit option built in.

Are you an appointment-setter?

No. I focus on lower-volume, higher-touch outreach where targeting, tone, and judgment matter as much as outcomes.

What are your outreach methods?

Methods vary by context, but typically include thoughtful phone calls, highly targeted emails, and coordinated scheduling.

Are you available for consulting?

Yes, on a limited basis, focused on messaging, targeting, packaging, and re-engaging existing accounts.
